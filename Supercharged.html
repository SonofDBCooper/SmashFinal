Supercharged is a pulse-pounding racing game that challenges players to navigate treacherous tracks and outmaneuver their opponents. With realistic physics and cutting-edge graphics, Supercharged is a true test of speed and skill. Can you handle the heat and cross the finish line first?

Customizable Cars

Choose from a range of high-performance vehicles and customize them with a variety of parts and upgrades.
